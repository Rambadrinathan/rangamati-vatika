08 Objection Handling Playbook

"What if leases don't sell?"
Your investment is backed by land. Ganga frontage only appreciates. Worst case: you own 24 Kathas of riverfront land.
"Why not use a bank loan?"
Banks fund post-approval construction. We need pre-construction capital for the Showcase Pod, which drives pre-leasing.
"No real estate track record."
30 years of building biophilic structures. 10+ completed projects including 15,000 sq.ft. Durga Puja pavilion. We build in-house.
"18-24 months seems fast."
Phase 1 (Showcase Pod) is 6 months. Pre-leasing starts immediately after. We've delivered similar-scale projects before.
"I want guaranteed returns."
No honest promoter guarantees returns. But your downside is protected: you own 25% of appreciating Ganga frontage.
"Who else has invested?"
KarmYog has ₹70 Lakhs of our own money in. We eat our own cooking.
"Bengal is not a hot market."
Exactly. That's why 97 Kathas of Ganga frontage costs ₹70L, not ₹7 Crores. You're investing before the market catches up.
"What legal structure?"
25% equity + 25% land registered in your name. Units on 99-year lease. Full legal documentation available.

15 Follow-Up Protocol

The 3-Touch Rule
  1. Day 1: Thank-you message + add value (new render or market data)
  2. Day 7: Check-in + share something new (podcast, article, progress photo)
