The Vision

RangaMati Vatika is a purpose-built intergenerational community on the banks of the Ganga — where children learn alongside elders, where eco-retreat guests experience Bengal's living heritage, and where 75 families hold 99-year leases on 1,000 sq.ft. units at just ₹40 Lakhs each. This is not a real estate project — it is a model for how India's next generation of communities should live.
